Summary

Black Duck, a privately held company founded in 1997, develops and markets evidence-based nutraceuticals for eye health.

The formulations, created by leading ophthalmologists, nutrition scientists and researchers, are based on the latest science and are recommended by thousands of ophthalmologists and optometrists nationwide.
